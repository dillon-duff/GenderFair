Our Methodology

Here at Gender Fair, we are committed to transparency and providing you with a clear understanding of how we calculate our fairness ratings for non-profit organizations. Below, you'll find a detailed breakdown of our methodology:

Factors We Consider

Leadership Diversity

We examine the gender and racial composition of the organization's board of directors and senior leadership team. A strong commitment to diversity at the top level signals an inclusive culture.

Pay Equity

We analyze salary data to assess any disparities in compensation between genders and racial groups. Fair pay practices are fundamental to an equitable workplace.

Data Sources

We utilize a combination of publicly available datasets and proprietary information to compile our rankings. Our primary data sources include:

  • Guidestar/Candid
  • Charity Navigator
  • IRS Form 990
